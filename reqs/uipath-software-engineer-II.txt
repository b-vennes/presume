Your Mission

Are you passionate about transforming data into actionable insights that drive automation decisions? Join our Insights team where you'll build powerful analytics solutions that help organizations understand and optimize their automation journey. Your mission will be to develop scalable, data-driven applications that combine intuitive frontend experiences with robust backend processing. At UiPath, we value quality, agility, and innovation as we create platforms that reveal the true potential of business process automation.

What you'll do at UiPath

    Design and develop fullstack solutions analytics and insights platform

    Create interactive data visualizations and dashboards that make complex information accessible

    Build scalable backend services that process and analyze automation performance data

    Implement APIs that connect various data sources to deliver comprehensive insights

    Ensure high availability of insights services through monitoring and continuous improvement

    Collaborate with data scientists and business analysts to translate requirements into technical solutions

    Participate actively in agile planning, estimation, and delivery processes

What you'll bring to the team

Required:

    Strong proficiency in .NET development with solid computer science fundamentals

    Experience building and deploying data-focused applications on Azure cloud services

    Demonstrated skills in React and modern frontend data visualization libraries

    Experience with RESTful API design and implementation for data services

    Ability to work effectively with globally distributed teams

Nice-to-have:

    Experience with business intelligence tools and analytics platforms

    Knowledge of data processing pipelines and big data technologies

    Familiarity with machine learning integration in web applications
